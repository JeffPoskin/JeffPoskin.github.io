Jeff Poskin

mathematician @ boeing

I am a mathematician in the Applied Math Group inside of Boeing Research & Technology. My research interests lie in the applications of integer programming and optimization to problems in aerospace engineering.

I received my PhD from the department of mathematics at the University of Wisconsin - Madison in May 2017 under the supervision of Professor Alberto Del Pia. My thesis work considered representability questions in mixed integer quadratic programming. I completed my undergraduate at the University of Kansas, where I majored in mathematics.
