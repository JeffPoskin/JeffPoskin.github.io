Jeff Poskin

researcher @ Boeing Global Services

I am a PhD candidate in the department of mathematics at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. I completed my undergraduate at the University of Kansas, where I majored in mathematics. My research interests lie in integer programming, optimization, and real algebraic geometry. Currently, I am considering representability questions in mixed integer quadratic programming. My advisor is Professor Alberto Del Pia.
